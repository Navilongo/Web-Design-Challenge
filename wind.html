Max Windspeed

From the wind speed plot, we see that the wind speed decreases the closer we are to the equator but as we move away, we begin seeing higher wind speeds. As a result of the wind speeds we likely see how the artic winds have been affecting the US recently and the further we are from the equator (the more north we are) the higher the wind speeds and how more rapidly the cold has been impacting different cities.
